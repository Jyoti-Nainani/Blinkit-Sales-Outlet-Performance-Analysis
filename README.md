# 🛒Blinkit Sales & Outlet Performance Analysis
## 📌Project Overview
This project analyzes Blinkit's sales performance, outlet characteristics, and product-level trends using Power BI. The objective is to uncover key drivers of revenue, identify high-performing segments, and recommend actionable business strategies.
## 🎯Problem Statement
Blinkit operates across multiple oultet types, sizes, and locations.
The goal is to:

- Identify which outlets and categories drive the most sales.
- Understand customer purchase behaviour.
- Provide strategic recommendations to improve revenue and efficiency.

## 🔍Key Insights

- Tier 3 cities contribute the highest sales, indicating strong untapped demand.
- Fresh produce (fruits & vegetables) drives the highest sales →→ high-frequency purchases.
- Medium- sized outlets have the highest order volume.
- Supermarket Type 1 generates the highest revenue →→ most efficient format.
- Low-fat category has lower contribution →→ potential growth segment.

## 🚀Strategic Recommendations

- Expand presence in **Tier 3 cities** with medium- sized outlets.
- Strengthen **fresh produce supply chain** to support high-frequency demand.
- Scale **Supermarket Type 1** model for better revenue efficiency.
- Introduce **category-based bundling** (e.g. fruits + dairy) to increase basket size.
- Expand **low-fat product assortment** to target health-conscious consumers.
